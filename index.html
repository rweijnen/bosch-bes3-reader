Use at your own risk

BES3 Reader is an independent, unofficial tool and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or supported by Bosch. It communicates directly with your eBike's drive unit.

  • You use this tool entirely at your own risk. The authors accept no liability for any damage, data loss, voided warranty, or malfunction.
  • Connecting to your drive unit may affect your manufacturer warranty or violate local regulations. You are responsible for how you use it.
  • This tool is almost entirely read-only. The exceptions are a small number of opt-in actions: a "reset to default" button per assist mode (resets that mode's assist level/max speed/acceleration response to factory defaults), and an "always start in last-used mode" button. Neither runs without you clicking it and confirming a dialog that explains exactly what it does.
  • Cloud lookups (when available) run against Bosch services using your own account; respect their terms of service.
